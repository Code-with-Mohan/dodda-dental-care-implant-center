Dr. Dodda Venkatesh Kumar, M.D.S

BDS, MDS(Prosthodontist & Implantologist)

Dr. Dodda Venkatesh Kumar, M.D.S, one of the leading Dentist in Narasaraopet and the brains behind Dodda Dental Care has curated and put forth a one-of-a-kind dental center catering to all kinds of dental troubles from kids to older adults. Adapting to modern treatments and catering excellent care with a well-versed team of dental experts is Dodda Dental Care's forte. Dr. Dodda Venkatesh Kumar completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery from St.Joseph's Dental College in Duggirala, Andhra Pradesh. Following this, he completed his Master's in Prosthodontics & Crowns and Bridges from Drs. Sudha & Nageswara Rao Siddhartha Institute of Dental Sciences in Gannavaram, Andhra Pradesh.

Dodda Dental Care is a specialized dental clinic in Narasaraopet that maintains rich quality & high standards of treatment. Our highly experienced team of the best dentists in Narasaraopet can easily address your dental needs and treat them accordingly. Personalized patient care is what makes our clinic unique. Every patient can get the required dental services from our caring clinical staff. Dodda Dental Care Dental Clinic will always strive hard to bring a happy & confident smile to every patient face.

We aim to provide outstanding dental care with a commitment to quality and integrity & honesty. Our goal is to be widely recognized as the leading and most preferred Dental Care center. At Dodda Dental Care, the complete focus is on patient treatment by putting in all possible efforts. Moreover, our highest ethical standards, assure the highest quality dental treatments.

Our dedicated team includes prosthodontists and implantologist, oral and maxillofacial surgeons, specialists in oral medicine, experts in public health, endodontists, periodontists, orthodontists, and pedodontists. Our procedures include full mouth rehabilitation, painless laser treatments, Crowns, bridges, implants, complete dentures, implant-supported complete dentures, root canal treatment, fillings, impactions, scaling and root planing, Flap surgery, braces, Invisalign, custom-made sports guards, etc. Moreover, Our dental clinic is well known not only for high standards of treatments but also for safety & hygiene. We carry out proper Sterilization methods for every dental tool used in our treatment as a part of our hygiene process.

Our Dental Clinic team follows covid precautions and keeps themselves protected by Personal Protective Equipment which includes full Body Coverall, Hand Gloves, Shoe Covers, Face Masks, and Face Shield. We regularly sanitize our clinic & equipment to assure safe treatments for the patients.
