FAQ's

Why do so many people choose dental implants?

Dental implants are the most natural looking and natural feeling replacement for missing teeth. Unlike other tooth replacement options, dental implants don't slip or click while you talk, laugh or eat. Also, they assist with keeping your jawbone healthy, so that your facial structure stays intact.

How many teeth can be replaced with dental implants?

You can replace a single tooth, multiple teeth or all of your teeth with implants. You don't even need one implant for each missing tooth. As few as two implants can support a removable lower denture, while as few as four implants can give a full, permanent set of top or bottom teeth.

How might my child develop healthy oral hygiene habits?

Brushing can be fun! We're delighted to assist your kid with finding the significance of healthy teeth! We teach parents and kids about proper brushing techniques. Flossing is additionally a significant part of good oral hygiene habits, and we'll let you know when your kid ought to begin flossing.

How is TMJ disorder detected?

There are a several symptoms that can indicate an issue with the TMJ: bruxism, jaw pain, headaches and limited mouth opening, among others.

What are the Advantages of Laser gum disease Therapy?

Laser treatment for gum disease advances quick healing of the tissues and, since it is a minimally invasive procedure, won't ordinarily cause gum recession. It can really save teeth that would be extracted or at a higher risk of damage after traditional gum surgery.

Am I a candidate for Laser Gum Surgery?

Patients with moderate to severe Periodontal (gum) disease can particularly benefit from the LANAP protocol. The LANAP protocol is also an excellent treatment choice for patients who are afraid of conventional scalpel surgery.
